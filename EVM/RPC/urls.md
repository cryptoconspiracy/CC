# Privacy-Focused RPC Resources

A curated list of RPC providers and network discovery tools, with a focus on privacy, MEV protection, and reliable EVM network connections.

---

## 1. 1RPC  
**Link:** https://www.1rpc.io/  

1RPC is one of the most trusted privacy-oriented RPC providers in the crypto ecosystem.  
It has been running for a long time without collecting user transaction data, making it a strong choice for users who want anonymity and protection from tracking or profiling.

**Key points:**
- Free to use  
- Supports a large number of EVM networks  
- Focused on privacy and data minimization  

---

## 2. LlamaNodes  
**Link:** https://llamanodes.com/pt-br  

Created by the DeFi Llama team, LlamaNodes is another privacy-focused RPC provider.  
Although it supports fewer networks compared to 1RPC, the service is growing fast and has become a trusted alternative.

**Key points:**
- Free tier available  
- Strong privacy focus  
- Limited number of networks for now  
- Backed by a reputable team (DeFi Llama)  

---

## 3. ChainList  
**Link:** https://chainlist.org/  

ChainList is a network discovery tool also created by the DeFi Llama ecosystem.  
It does **not** provide RPCs directly — instead, it aggregates RPC endpoints from many chains so users can easily add networks to their wallets.

**Key points:**
- Huge list of EVM-compatible networks  
- Useful for quickly adding networks to MetaMask, Rabby, etc.  
- Prefer privacy-oriented RPCs whenever available  

---

## 4. ChainID Network  
**Link:** https://chainid.network/  

A community alternative to ChainList.  
Provides chain IDs, RPC endpoints, and metadata for many networks.

**Key points:**
- Good alternative resource for developers and users  
- Useful when ChainList is missing details  
- Community-maintained  

---

## Summary

Choosing the right RPC is not just a performance decision — it's a **security and privacy** decision.  
Whenever possible, prefer RPCs focused on anonymity, MEV protection, and user safety.
