# DeFi Lending Protocols — Networks & Official Links (2025)

This document lists the major DeFi lending protocols with their official websites and supported networks.  
Source reference: DeFiLlama (https://defillama.com/protocols/lending)

---

## 1. Aave  
**Website:** https://aave.com  
**Supported Networks:**  
- Ethereum  
- Polygon  
- Arbitrum  
- Optimism  
- Avalanche  
- Base  
- Gnosis Chain  
- Scroll  

Aave remains the most widely deployed and battle-tested lending protocol in DeFi.

---

## 2. Morpho (Morpho Blue + MetaMorpho)  
**Website:** https://morpho.org  
**Supported Networks:**  
- Ethereum (only)

Morpho focuses on efficiency, safety and isolated vault design.  
Institutional-grade lending, fully concentrated on Ethereum mainnet.

---

## 3. JustLend  
**Website:** https://justlend.io  
**Supported Networks:**  
- TRON (native network)

The dominant lending platform on TRON, serving the ecosystem similarly to how Aave serves Ethereum.

---

## 4. SparkLend (Maker Ecosystem)  
**Website:** https://sparkprotocol.io  
**Supported Networks:**  
- Ethereum

Spark is deeply integrated with DAI and Maker’s architecture, hence remaining Ethereum-only.

---

## 5. Maple Finance  
**Website:** https://maple.finance  
**Supported Networks:**  
- Ethereum  
- Solana  
- Base

Maple specializes in undercollateralized and institutional credit markets.

---

## 6. Compound Finance  
**Website:** https://compound.finance  
**Supported Networks:**  
- Ethereum (V2)  
- Ethereum (V3 USDC markets)  
- Base (V3)

A foundational lending protocol in DeFi, widely integrated and extensively audited.

---

## 7. Venus Protocol  
**Website:** https://venus.io  
**Supported Networks:**  
- BNB Chain (native)

The largest lending protocol on BNB Chain, similar to Compound/Aave structure.

---

## 8. Euler Finance  
**Website:** https://euler.finance  
**Supported Networks:**  
- Ethereum

After its relaunch, Euler operates exclusively on Ethereum for risk-control reasons.

---

## 9. Gearbox Protocol  
**Website:** https://gearbox.fi  
**Supported Networks:**  
- Ethereum  
- Arbitrum

Provides leverage-as-a-service through composable credit accounts.

---

## 10. Fraxlend (Frax Finance)  
**Website:** https://frax.finance  
**Supported Networks:**  
- Ethereum  
*(Some vaults interact with multichain Frax Ferry infrastructure.)*

Fraxlend offers isolated markets and is tightly integrated with the broader Frax ecosystem.

---

## 11. Moonwell  
**Website:** https://moonwell.fi  
**Supported Networks:**  
- Base  
- Moonbeam  
- Moonriver  
- Optimism  

Moonwell is an open lending and borrowing protocol focused on the Polkadot/Kusama and L2 ecosystem, originally built for Moonbeam and Moonriver, and later expanded to Base and Optimism. It emphasizes security (multiple audits, bug bounty) and a very accessible UI for onchain lending. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
