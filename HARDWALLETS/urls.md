## 2. Tangem  
**Link:** https://redirect.sale/tangem/?promocode=BWPMBP  

Tangem is a card-shaped hardware wallet — extremely portable and resistant.  
It stores private keys in a secure NFC chip, allowing you to sign transactions using your phone by tapping the card.  
There are no cables, no batteries, no screens to break.  
It’s one of the simplest hardware wallets ever made and very popular for people who want a “tap-to-sign” experience with strong security.

## 3. Ledger  
**Link:** https://shop.ledger.com/pages/referral-program?referral_code=3084F9KNY5RG3  

Ledger is the most widely recognized hardware wallet brand in the world.  
It supports thousands of cryptocurrencies and integrates with almost every major wallet app.  
Models like Nano S Plus and Nano X are known for strong security, long-term durability, and broad ecosystem support.  
Ledger is the industry standard for people who want maximum compatibility with Web3 and DeFi.
