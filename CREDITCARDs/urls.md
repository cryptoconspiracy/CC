# General Notes About Crypto Debit Cards

Crypto debit cards convert your crypto into fiat at the time of purchase, allowing you to spend them like regular money.

**Common benefits include:**  
- Cashback  
- Travel insurance perks  
- Airport lounge access  
- Global acceptance  
- ATM withdrawals  
- Stablecoin-based spending  

**Always check:**  
- FX spread and conversion fees  
- Daily and monthly limits  
- Supported regions  
- Stability and reputation of the issuer  
- Whether the card is custodial or non-custodial  

---

# Safety Recommendations

### Before using any crypto card:
- Test with a **small amount** first.  
- Verify **global acceptance** for your destination country.  
- Read all **fees, limits, and fine print**.  
- Avoid storing large balances on custodial cards.  
- Understand how quickly funds convert from crypto → fiat.  
- Confirm if virtual and physical cards behave differently.

Crypto cards are extremely useful, but each comes with unique tradeoffs — understanding them ensures safe and efficient use.
